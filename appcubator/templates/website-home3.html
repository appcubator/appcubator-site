More Info
What can I build?

Almost anything you use daily on the web. This includes marketplaces, Airbnb type sites, online ordering apps or social networks.

Unfortunately, games or flash based technologies are not supported.
Who is it for?

It is for everyone. If you can use Powerpoint and Excel, you can use Appcubator. It is designed with non-technical people in mind but we would like add special features for designers and developers soon.
How is it possible?

We did most of the heavy lifting for you and integrated all the components of popular apps out there for you. You just need to define what information you would like to save and then go build your what you have in mind through a powerful drag'n'drop interface.
