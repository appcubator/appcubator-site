About Us

Appcubator is designed for entrepreneurs who want to publish custom web applications with no coding skills required. We believe that everyone deserves to connect with their customers using the convenience of a web browser. Our founders Karan Sikka, Ilter Canberk, and Nikhil Khadke have developed a system that with the press of a button develops and deploys the code required for the site. Appcubator provides the ultimate in custom app development as it is the only platform to give the user the code.

What are you waiting for? Let us do the coding for you!
