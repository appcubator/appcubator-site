More Info
What can I build?

Everything you use daily like the social networks you check throughout the day, the online ordering solutions at lunchtime, and the community marketplaces you use to plan weekend getaways.

Currently, games and flash-based technologies are not supported.
Who is it for?

Appcubator is for everyone. It is designed with non-technical people in mind; if you can use Powerpoint and Excel, you can use Appcubator. We will also be releasing an advanced mode for designers and developers soon.
How is it possible?

We did most of the heavy lifting for you and integrated the features and functions of popular apps. You just need to define what information you would like to save and then go build what you have in mind through our powerful interface editor.
