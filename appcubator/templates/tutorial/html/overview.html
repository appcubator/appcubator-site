"What's an application?", you might ask. We want to give you an overview of how things work on a running applcation, no matter how big or small it is.

A web application like a restaurant. There are many different parts to it, such as:

  • The presentation: How things look is an important part of any business. If things look good and elegant, it's likely that you will have more customers and a better business.
  • Storage: You need to store things, such as the food, somewhere in your restaurant so that you can serve them when it's the time.
  • Customer Input: You need to learn what your customers want so that you can serve the right thing.
