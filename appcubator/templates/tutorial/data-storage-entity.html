You can think of an entity as an Excel spreadsheet. If you want to show a list of documents, for example, you need to have a `Document` entity. Any type of important informatino that is central to your application should have an entity likewise. Photo, Tweet, Post, Game are some very popular samples.
After you identify important entities in your app, you should add their properties, just like the columns on an Excel table.
Users are also entities, but they have exceptional properties.
